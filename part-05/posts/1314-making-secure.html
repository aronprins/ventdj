Making secure

10/7/2010


The head of this figure is made of cast neoprene which, in its cured state, is still slightly flexible. The mouth axle and eye mount help stabilize the face portion of the head, but the rear of the head has no such inner supports making it a bit difficult to perfectly line up the more flexible edges of the "door". To easily remedy this situation, I glued sixpositioning tabs along the inside edges of the door. When the door is then taped into place (duct tape), it is much more stable and secure.
