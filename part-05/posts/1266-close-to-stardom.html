Close to stardom!

11/12/2010




Thanks to Phil Nichols, one of my smiley face pocket puppets made it to Hollywood's "Walk of Fame"! On his visit to Hollywood and the Walk of Fame, Phil paused to take a photo of himself with Edgar Bergen's star, first pulling out his handy Pocket Puppet to pose with him. The visit was brief, but to date, that's about as close to stardom as any puppet I've touched has come!
