For Sale: Flexible latex hands by Lovik

12/6/2010


This is a rare and somewhat unusual pair of hands for a ventriloquist figure. Designed by Craig Lovik for a 38"-40" figure, they are made of flexible latex, and designed to be a temporary fix on a figure with broken or missing hand, because they are made of flexible latex and can be slipped on and off like a glove/mitten. I suppose they could be used as a permanent hand on a figure although the wrist would be a bit shorter than I would like if used for that purpose - the overall length of this pair is 5 1/2". I don't think this pair was ever used. For sale Here.
