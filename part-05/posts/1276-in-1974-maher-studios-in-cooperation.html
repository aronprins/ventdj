Untitled

11/5/2010





In 1974 Maher Studios, in cooperation with members of the NAAV, published more than 2 dozen books of original ventriloquist dialogue. Today, thanks to Bob Abdou (who sent me a box of his duplicates) I have two copies of Dialogue Book 27 to give away.
This out-of-print book contains routines written by William Andersen, Marty Mann (2 routines), Sherry Gosse, Dave L. Mund (2 birthday party routines), and Joyce Mongomery. (I'd love to hear from any of you authors if you are reading this!)
The winners of these two books are: Jerry Barrilleaux and Bill Leach.
