Signed, Framed Shop Photo

12/1/2010


I truly believe there was only one time in the 40 years I have been building ventriloquist figures when I had a dozen of my own completed dummies in my shop at one and the same time. And that is when this photo was taken a couple years back.
My normal procedure is to pack and ship figures as they are completed rather than keep them hanging around. But on this one occasion I worked to bring a full dozen to completion simultaneously, just for a once-in-a-lifetime photo. I sign and date the headpost on each figure which is what the photo shows.
