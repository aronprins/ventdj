Taking the edge off

10/5/2010


As I worked on this fibre glass body, I discovered the edges were thin and sharp - not good for the wrist of a performer! So I took a length of 1/4" O.D. plastic tube (drip line for an irrigation system), split it for the full length, slipped it over the edge, and glued it in place with "super glue". Problem solved.
