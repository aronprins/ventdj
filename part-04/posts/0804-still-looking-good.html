From Mr. D: You have one of the figures from Craig Lovik's line of Storyteller figures from the '70s. All those figures featured the "living mouth" (non-slotted). At first I thought this was the "Randy" character, but after studying it a bit, I'm not so sure. But it appears to be in nice condition after nearly 40 years. Congratulations on your purchase.
