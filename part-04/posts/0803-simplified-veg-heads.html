Simplified "Veg Heads"

9/2/2011

From Kirk Rabe


Supplies: Foam Boards, scissors, craft glue, googly eyes, band aids, crafts sticks.


1) Cut fruit shapes (You can draw pattern first if you like)


2) Cut out square mouth. Be sure to make mouth slightly smaller (TRIM EDGES).


3) Glue Mouth only to craft stick.


4) Attach band aid to back of fruit.


5) Slide craft stick into band aid gap.


6) Cut out another small strip for holding the puppet with finger. Attach that with two more band aids.


7) Decorate fruit and have FUN!
