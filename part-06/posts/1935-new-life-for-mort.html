New life for Mort

12/2/2009



This (left) is one of the original Mortimer dolls manufactured by Juro. He was sent to me for upgrading:
Hollow body,
Head on headpost,
Lever controls,
Side-to-side eyes,
Raising eyebrows,
Larger body,
Deluxe legs,
Repainted,
Wig and eyelashes.
A speedy and very cost efficient way to achieve a nice semi-pro figure (right). He didn't have far to travel either, since he resides in neighboring Boulder, Colorado! I hope to be seeing you around town, Mort!
