We have our winners!

12/15/2009


Here's a collector's item for you! A mint condition copy of The New Oracle (Mark Wade editor and publisher) May-June 1984 with Jeff Dunham and his McElroy "Ollie" the cover feature. I notice when Mark was writing about his featured vent for this issue he closed his writing with these words: "Jeff and "Ollie" have done much in a short time and we're sure you'll see much more out of this very talented young ventriloquist." How prophetic!
This issue also has articles by Gary Owen, Bill DeMar & Conrad Hartz. News and photos of numerous other vents, ads & more.

I sent a copy FREE to the first eleven (11) people who responed to this post. Congratulations all.
