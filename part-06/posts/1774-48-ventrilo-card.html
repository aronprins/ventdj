Judy Buch
(Set #4, Card #12)

Judy Buch was presenting a workshop in Washington D.C. for a national teacher's conference in 1982 when she met Cathy Gibbons of "LAUGHMAKERS' magazine. Cathy influenced Judy to take the Maher home study course to enrich her music education program. In 1988, Judy performed at the Ventriloquist Convention's Night Before Show, and 1989, the Friday Night Show.

Judy is a full-time working professional, who has given more than 1,000 school performances, and toured with the New England New Vaudeville Review.

Judy is a keynote speaker and uses the art of ventriloquism for corporations. She performs her 90-minute one-woman show in theaters in New England, and co-sponsors Ventriloquist's Association of New England. Her goal is to help ventriloquists increase their originality and to elevate the art form. (Copyright 1995 OO-LA-LA, INK.)
