Mother's Day report

5/11/2009


When I told a friend I took Adelia to the Colorado Rockies baseball game yesterday as her Mother's Day gift, my friend replied, "Was that a gift for her or for you?" To which I replied, "You don't know my wife very well, do you?" Yes, I'm married to a sports fan - we had a great afternoon. And the Rockies won, which made it even better!
