Maher Library "Down Under"

4/6/2009

I had a fun project last week. A customer from Australia ordered one copy each of all the Maher Publications still in print - 147 total! Pulling that order and checking and rechecking titles was a sizable job. And shipping two heavy parcels by air post to the other side of the world was a sizable cost! You won't hear me complain about a 44 cent stamp next month! (Well, at least not for a day or two.)

(You can find a list and description of all Maher Publications here: http://www.maherbookstore.com/ )
