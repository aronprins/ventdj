Two residents

4/4/2010


Here are two smiling Hillsdale Gardens (Thunder Bay, ON) residents, Canon John Jordan and his friend Wilfred (better known as Willy) getting ready to go down and visit with the others in the dining-room. So far, the visiting has been table to table, a little chat, leaning close to those who are hard of hearing, and then moving along. Not surprisingly, John says there are numerous "When are we going to see Willy again?" questions. If Hillsdale Gardens ever has a "favorite resident" contest, John has a "ringer" he can enter!
