Great Ventriloquist Give-away

2/11/2010

In 1995 M. A. Denemark produced his hugely popular sets of Great Ventriloquist Ventrilo-cards. Four sets of 12 photo cards, plus trivia and cover cards. On the back of each card is a brief bio of the ventriloquist featured in the black and white photo on the front. These have long been sold out and are today truly ventriloquist collector cards. However, thanks to M.A., I now have several sets for give-away on this blog. The cards came from M.A.'s personal stock - a rare and generous gift.

So here's my plan. There are 48 different cards. Over the next several weeks I will individually give-away two each 48 ventrilo-cards, beginning today with card number 12 of set #4 and working forward to the number one card of set #1.

Each card will be matted and mounted in a 5x7 frame, and come with a duplicate loose card. Good luck and enjoy!
