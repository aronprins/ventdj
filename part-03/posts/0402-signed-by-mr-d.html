Signed by Mr. D.

5/5/2012

When Bruce Fife published this book in 1989, he requested numerous photos from Steve Axtell and myself to be used on the pages this book to illustrate the various aspects of ventriloquism being taught through the written words.  I've signed one of my photos (page 43).  Whether that makes this copy more or less special - I'll leave that for the buyer to decide.  I can tell you the 22 comedy dialogues making up the back half of the book make this work something to seriously consider if you are looking for comedy material for your shows.  And, it goes without saying, the teaching of Paul Stadleman is legendary!
