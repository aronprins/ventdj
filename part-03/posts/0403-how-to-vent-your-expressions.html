How To Vent Your Expressions

5/5/2012

Comment:  It's too bad that wonderful book by Cliff Taylor, How To Vent Your Expressions,  is out of print.  Maybe I could find it through a local bookstore used book program.  I have gotten a number of ventriloquist books that were out of print that way. 
*  *  *  *  *
From Mr. D: How to Vent Your Expressions is an amazing work - just look up any attitude or emotion, and the author, who was a master at figure manipulation, explains how to move the figure to show that specific attitude or emotion through the figure's movements.  This is a big book and costly to print (comparatively), but I do have the master and would be willing to run off a few copies if a few others reading this were interested in purchasing a copy ($10.00)  I doubt you would find this book at a local bookstore since it was published in very, very limited number.
