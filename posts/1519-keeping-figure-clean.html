Keeping the figure clean

6/12/2010

Question: What's the best way to keep the figure clean and fresh? Should it be kept in some kind of bag? And is it okay to leave the head in place, or should it be removed when the figure is stored? Lastly, what about the wig? How do you keep it clean and nice? David

* * * * *
Answer: The best way to keep a figure clean and safe is to keep it in a padded suitcase (with head in place) when not practicing or performing. Cover the face and head with a soft fabric material such as an old T-Shirt. (Many use towels, but terry cloth tends to muss the hair more than material with smoother nap.) Slip a pair of socks over the hands. It's probably obvious, but always wash your hands and dry thoroughly before handling the figure. Mr. D
