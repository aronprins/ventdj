"From here, nearly everywhere

11/2/2011

Adelia and I are Hesston College alumni, and the school has a trademark phrase: Start here, go everywhere.  I've often thought I should hang the same or similar slogan over my shop door.  Because that's exactly what takes place with the puppets and figures built within the four walls of that small building.  They start there, and from there, go more places than I would ever have imagined.  Just recently I started a photo listing of figures sold and where they went.  Even I am amazed as I watch the listing advance.  You can see them in the margin of the page: http://mrdsfigures.blogspot.com/
