Frisbee anyone?

9/3/2011





This FRISBEE is new (never thrown). As I recall I had 250 of these made. I don't remember the year. Early '90s perhaps. Only a handful remain (literally). 9" in diameter. Designed especially for "voice throwers" by the voice throwing specialists www.maherstudios.com/ I used to hand them to ventriloquists saying, "when you are tired of throwing your voice, go throw a Frisbee!" Now you find these in the hands of collectors - another fun piece of ventriloquism memorabilia - more valuable now on the display shelf than in the air!
