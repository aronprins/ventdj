Ronn Lucas - Boy's Life

4/4/2012

Boy's Life (my favorite magazine as a teen) has always be kind to ventriloquists and the art of ventriloquism.  The March, 2012 issue contains a brief article about Ronn Lucas, and features his instructions on how to be a ventriloquist.  Thank you Ronn and editors of Boy's Life...you're still on my list of "favorites".
