"Lots of dogs"


Here's my contribution to today's backyard BBQ - two framed "Lots of Dogs", an original Ventoon by artist Dave Miller. First published in the 1978 Ventriloquist Calendar published by the N.A.A.V. in cooperation with Maher Studios.

The winners of these 8x10 framed works are: John Iskat and Gary Koepke.

The ventriloquist figure featured in the Ventoon is "Friendly Freddy", well known for his appearance throughout the pages of the Maher 30 Lesson Course of Ventriloquism: http://mahercourse.blogspot.com
