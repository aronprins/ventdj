The inside back cover was always one of my favorite pages.

8/16/2011

The inside back cover was always one of my favorite pages. That's where Cartoonsville, featuring the clever art of fellow ventriloquists, was found and always added smiles to my day. Dialogue was a publication of Bob Ladd, Editor. Most of you will recognize Bob Rumba as the cover featured ventriloquist on this issue from the Summer of 1998.
