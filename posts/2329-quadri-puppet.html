Quadri-Puppet

4/1/2009


I've been building vent figures for ventriloquists for what will be exactly 40 years come August 1 this year. And just when I think I've had every request imaginable some new surprise awaits! Who could have imagined a vent figure with four arms! What would you name such a figure - "Armstrong"?
I suppose when finished this fellow could effectively be used to teach respect for people who appear to be different from ourselves. He could certainly point out the program's major thoughts - four times, in fact ... simultaneously!
(I just hope I'm not asked to install a handshaker - things could get out of hand ... )
