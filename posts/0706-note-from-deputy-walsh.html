Note from Deputy Walsh

11/2/2011

I just received a note from Deputy Bob Walsh who is currently on tour in China.  He just completed  a stretch of 20 school programs in four cities.  In the city of Kunming, someone broke into the Principal's office but none of his dummies were taken or damaged even though a lock was broken off his trunk.  Nothing of his was taken although some things moved around.  Bob thought the intruder must have thought they were just toys.  I'd prefer to think the dummies frightened the bad guy(s) away!  :)
