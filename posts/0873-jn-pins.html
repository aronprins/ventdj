J/N Pins

8/8/2011



Each convention attendee was given four of the smaller character pins - all alike. The idea was to make exchanges with others until you came up with the complete set of four different pins.


A great idea although I have to tell you, I might have been tempted to stop trading if I came up with two of Danny and two of Farfel. I'll admit it - they are my favorites!
