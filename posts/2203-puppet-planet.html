Puppet Planet

6/2/2009


For those of you who enjoy creatures of great quality and lots of whimsical characteristics we suggest that you take a few minutes to look at a site that is just full of wonderful characters. Michele Acquin's wonderful work can be found at her site. Take a minute and enjoy the tour at Puppet Planet

The puppet shown here is a stage puppet, but Puppet Planet provides 36" full body stock and custom puppets as well.
