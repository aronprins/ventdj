Easter Sunday 2009

4/12/2009


As a visitor to this blog you are special to me and I want to wish you a very

Happy Easter!

Easter has always been my favorite of all holidays, and not just because of colored eggs, chicks, bunnies and (in this case) dummies. I do enjoy all such reminders of the happy events surrounding the holiday, but they are meaningless when compared to the One reason for which I truly celebrate today:

Jesus is Alive!
"Jesus said to her, 'I am the resurrection and the life. Anyone who believes in me will live, even though they die; and whoever lives by believing in me will never die.'"
John 11:25-26 (TNIV)
