In his own words...

5/3/2010


By Fred Maher


"Ventriloquism is your art and mine. Any added effort you put into this ART, because of your love of it, will show forth and make it a great joy and happiness to those who have the privilege of being entertained by YOU, and your little 'fun-maker'. It is the 'added effort' and desire to do it just a little better TODAY than yesterday that will make you progress."
* * * * *
From Lesson 9 of the Fred Maher Home Study Course of Ventriloquism, copyright 1948, 2007.
