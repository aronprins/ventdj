Author Wanda E Brunstetter

9/4/2011



While perusing the display of new fiction releases at our local library last week, I spotted The Journey by Wanda E. Brunstetter. Thinking back to when we first met Wanda as a result of her ventriloquist hobby, I always smile when I see Wanda's books today alongside other New York Times bestsellers, and make it a point to read her uplifting and intriguing works, most often intertwined with the Amish lifestyle and a touch of romance.
