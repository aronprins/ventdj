Shop Talk

11/3/2011

The head in the foreground is one I started years ago and never completed (Plastic Wood construction).  Perhaps now is the time - I thought I'd check it over to see how big a job it would be, wondering if it would make a good "spare time" winter project.

The head hanging in the background is even older, an early '40s Charlie.  I have restored and repainted the head which originally was fitted with a stuffed body, but I decided  to mount it on a head post.  I'm finishing a body for it today.  Completed, it will not be as original, but it will be a very nice, unique one-of-a-kind.  I hope to have pictures very soon.
