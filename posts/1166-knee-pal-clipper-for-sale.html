Knee Pal "Clipper"

1/8/2011

This is somewhat of a "hodgepodge" collage of photos, but hopefully it will give you a good idea of how the refurbished "Clipper" figure appears today. Standard turning head, side-to-side self-centering eyes, open & close mouth, plus lowering eyebrows (the eyebrows do not raise), winker, blinkers, and flapping ears. Clipper has always been one of my personal favorite characters. My own "R. C." is one of them, but with slightly smaller head. The Clipper here came from the "master mold". My "R.C" came from a "copy mold". (Neither mold is still in existence.)
